About Me

Hey, I’m Joey Streeter 👋

So here’s the quick version: I’m Joey — a people-first professional who’s built a career around customer service, project coordination, and creating solutions that make life easier for others. Over the years, I’ve worn a lot of hats: helping folks in high-volume call centers, managing projects, supporting nonprofits, and even working in fashion retail and property management.

At the core of it all, I love connecting with people and finding ways to bridge the gap between what they need and how to actually make it happen. Whether it’s coordinating a big project, simplifying a process, or just being there for someone who needs help, that’s where I thrive.

Outside of work, I’ve got a creative side too — I’m into music, branding, and exploring how design can bring ideas to life. I try to approach everything with curiosity, empathy, and a “let’s figure this out” attitude. If there’s one thing people know me for, it’s that I bring warmth and professionalism to whatever space I’m in.

Always learning, always growing, and always open to new opportunities. 🚀
